Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Decodji ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our emoji puzzle game at decodji.com.

Information We Collect

Local Storage Data: We store game progress, scores, streaks, and preferences locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Analytics Data: We use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as page views, device type, and general location (country level). This helps us improve the game experience.

Third-Party Advertising

Google AdSense

This website uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

Information collected by Google may include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and settings
  • Device type and operating system
  • Referring URLs
  • Browsing activity on this and other sites
  • Interaction with advertisements

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, visit: Google Privacy Policy

Advertising Partners

Some of our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our advertising partners include:

  • Google AdSense

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology for advertisements and links that appear on Decodji. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons) may also be used by third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

We have no access to or control over cookies used by third-party advertisers. You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices.

Cookies

We and our advertising partners use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and game progress
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Deliver relevant advertisements
  • Measure advertising effectiveness

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect game functionality.

Data Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal game data on our servers. Third-party services (Ezoic, Vercel) maintain their own security practices.

Children's Privacy

Decodji is a general audience game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access to your data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Opting out of personalized advertising
  • Data portability

To clear all local game data, you can clear your browser's localStorage for this site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
