Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Decodji ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our emoji puzzle game at decodji.com.

Information We Collect

Local Storage Data: We store game progress, scores, streaks, and preferences locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Analytics Data: We use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as page views, device type, and general location (country level). This helps us improve the game experience.

Third-Party Advertising (Ezoic)

This website uses the services of Ezoic Inc. ("Ezoic"), including to manage third-party interest-based advertising.

Cookies and Tracking: Ezoic and its partners use first and third-party cookies to track your browsing activity. Tags, beacons, pixels, and similar technologies may be used to monitor ad interactions.

Data Collected: Information gathered may include:

  • IP address
  • Operating system and device type
  • Language preferences
  • Browser type
  • Unique IDs and advertising IDs
  • Geolocation data
  • Usage data and device information

Partner Data Sharing: Ezoic's partners may collect this data for audience segmentation across platforms.

User Controls: You can manage your ad preferences through the consent manager that appears when you first visit our site. Disabling cookies may restrict some content access. Rejecting cookies results in non-personalized ads rather than eliminating ads entirely.

For more information about Ezoic's privacy practices, visit: Ezoic Privacy Policy

View Ezoic's advertising partners: Ezoic Advertising Partners

Cookies

We and our advertising partners use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and game progress
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns
  • Deliver relevant advertisements
  • Measure advertising effectiveness

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect game functionality.

Data Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal game data on our servers. Third-party services (Ezoic, Vercel) maintain their own security practices.

Children's Privacy

Decodji is a general audience game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access to your data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Opting out of personalized advertising
  • Data portability

To clear all local game data, you can clear your browser's localStorage for this site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
