How to Play

Master the art of decoding emoji puzzles

The Basics

Decodji is simple to learn but endlessly entertaining. Each puzzle shows you a series of emojis that represent a movie, song, phrase, brand, or place. Your goal is to figure out what they represent and type your answer!

Getting Started

1

Look at the Emojis

Study the emoji clues carefully. Think about what each emoji represents and how they might combine to form a word, phrase, or title.

2

Check the Category

The category badge tells you what type of answer to look for - Movies, TV Shows, Songs, Phrases, Brands, or Places. This narrows down the possibilities!

3

Type Your Answer

Enter your guess in the text box and press Enter or tap the arrow button. Don't worry about exact spelling - we allow for minor typos!

4

Use Hints if Stuck

If you're stuck, tap the Hint button to reveal a letter. But use them wisely - hints reduce your score!

Tips for Success

  • Think literally AND figuratively: Sometimes emojis represent sounds (üêù = "B" sound) or visual concepts.
  • Consider the category: Knowing you're looking for a movie vs. a phrase changes how you interpret the emojis.
  • Look for patterns: Common emoji combinations often appear - learn to recognize them!
  • Don't overthink it: The answer is usually more obvious than you think.
  • Play daily: Regular play builds your emoji vocabulary and pattern recognition.
  • Save hints for hard puzzles: You have limited hints, so use them strategically.
